1. Overview

In January 1996, multiple civilian witnesses in Varginha reported encountering a non-human entity in an urban environment.

Brazilian authorities attributed events to misidentification and routine military activity.

Internal classification: Localized Entity Sighting with Military Containment Response.

Unlike remote-area UFO cases, this incident occurred in a populated city, increasing witness density and media exposure.



2. Civilian Encounter

Three young women reported observing:

- A crouched humanoid figure
- Brown, oily skin texture
- Large red eyes
- Visible cranial protrusions
- Apparent weakness or injury

The entity was described as emitting a strong ammonia-like odor.

Witnesses insisted it was neither animal nor human.



3. Military Response

Shortly after reports circulated:
